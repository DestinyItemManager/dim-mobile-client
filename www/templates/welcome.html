  1. Try to view your items in DIM. This is a protected feature so it will reuire you to sign into Bungie.net.
  2. Sign into Bungie.net with your PSN ID or Microsoft Account.
  3. Verify that the data you see is your Bunige.net Account information.
  4. Sign out of your Bungie.net account.
